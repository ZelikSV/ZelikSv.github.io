about me

2003-2007

Student

In 2003 I entered the Polytechnic College of the KTU as a locomotive of a mining enterprise. During the training in the technical school, I first met with a computer. At the lessons of computer science I met Pascal and was not particularly impressed because I did not see anything supernatural in it.

2007-2011

Student

In 2007, I entered the Dnepropetrovsk National University of Railway Transport named after Academician Lazaryan. At the university, I received the first impetus to programming. I studied MathCad, Exel, AutoCad. These programs have already quite pleased me with a nice interface and I realized that I want to work with a computer, but I did not yet understand in which direction.

2011-2018

Technology engineer

After graduating from university, I got a job in the Krivoy Rog locomotive depot, where I work for the moment. My duties included the organization of repair of electric locomotives, the management of the change of workers.

April 2017-Present

Frontend Developer

When I got a job there was not much time to look for myself as a programmer. But the years went by and I realized that it was not mine. The first steps in my search were prompted by my younger brother. He advised me to look at java, which I started to do. Later I tried C #. But not one of them did not give me a sense of freedom. And then I tried my first steps towards the frontend. This was a real discovery for me. I found courses where the already functioning frontend developer taught the correct layout. He discovered the world of frontend for me.

Knowledge of HTML, CSS(Styling the site, creating and working with animation, SASS preprocessor, work with sprites), JQuery(UI), Ajax, basic knowledge of Javascript, Photoshop (PSD to HTML), Git, Gulp, Bootstrap4, adaptive, cross-browser and validated layout of sites, making changes to existing sites based on WordPress.
